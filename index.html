Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27/01/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Vocab handles data when you use the app. If you have any questions, contact us at focustimer25@gmail.com.

Information the app uses

Words you enter

When you type a word and tap Translate, the word and selected languages are sent to our backend to generate a translation, example sentence, and tips.

Saved words

When you save a word, it is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or sync saved words to our servers.

Usage limits

The app stores daily usage counters locally on your device to enforce free usage limits.

We do not store payment information.

What we do NOT collect

  • No ads or tracking SDKs

Data retention
